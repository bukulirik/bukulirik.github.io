12. She said that she doesn’t even know you at all / she didn’t even know you at all Which one is the right grammar


Jawaban:

the frist one. She said that "she doesn't even know you at all"

Jawaban:

The second sentence is correct, SHE SAID THAT SHE DIDN'T EVEN KNOW YOU AT ALL.

Penjelasan:

The keyword is the using of REPORTING VERB (SAID). If you use the past reporting verb, the noun clause should be in the similar structure. If you use the past reporting verb (SAID), the noun clause should be in THE SIMPLE PAST OR EVEN THE PAST PERFECT FORM, the tenses related to the past.

MEANWHILE, if you use the present reporting verb (SAYS), the noun clause should be in THE PRESENT OR EVEN PRESENT PERFECT FORM.
