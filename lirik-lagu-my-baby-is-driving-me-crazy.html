3. OH LORD.... MY BABY IS DRIVING ME CRAZY​


The phrase above seems to be uttered in a certain circumstance. Such would mostly be said in a situation when one felts annoyed or is whining / complaining about the tiring stuff he/she's been experiencing.

Penjelasan:

Such phrase based on the exercise above is said to be a common spontaneous whining expression when someone feels infuriated or unhappy with the situation, especially related to his/ her 'baby', who can be referring to his/her lover or spouse or can be about his/her children.

Here are the details of the meaning of each word or phrases above:

Oh Lord (means 'Oh God' artinya 'Ya Tuhan'. 'Lord' here means the Al-Mighty God in Christianity context)driving me crazy mad ( it can mean 'infuriate' and 'annoy' me. Another way of saying it is 'It is driving me mad, annoys me or is ticking me off)

34. karto said to Karno " you are driving me crazy" jawabannya apa ya​


Jawabannya adalah..
=> Dilihat dari kalimat direct nya menggunakan PRESENT CONTINUOUS :

S + To be (am/are/is) + V-ing + O
=> Karto Said to Karno "You ARE DRIVING me crazy".

Maka indirect nya menggunakan PAST CONTINUOUS
S + To be (was/were) + V-ing + O
=> Karto said to Karno that He WAS DRIVING him crazy.
