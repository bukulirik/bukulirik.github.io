Kebahagian itu tak perlu dicari. Kebahagiaan itu ada dan tidak jauh. Kebahagiaan itu ada di dalam hati. Kebahagiaan itu dekat. English : The lyrics of this song tell a story of someone who longs for true happiness. Even though people enjoy happiness that is obtained from wallowing, from fame, from power, or even having fun. But what does it all mean if there's no one on the side to lean on and share stories.
